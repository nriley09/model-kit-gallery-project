EXM-A9s Spinatio (Sengoku Type)

Brand: 30 Minute Missions

Tools I Used on The Build:

  • GodHand Ultimate Nipper 5.0
  • Bauer 800 Grit Sandpaper
  • Bauer 1200 Grit Sandpaper
  • Exacto Knife
  • Tweezers
  • Polishing Cloth

Thoughts:

This is first non-Gundam model kit I built! I saw this kit at Hobby Lobby and was interested in buying something besides a Gundam. I found this kit pretty interesting as the 30 Minute Missions line of model kits have customizable armor you can add to a kit. I only had an issue with the shoulder armor because it limited the kit's ability to be posed. Overall, I think the 30 Minute Missions brand has the ability to give Gundams some much needed competition
