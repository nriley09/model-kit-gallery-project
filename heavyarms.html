HGAC Gundam Heavyarms

Tools I Used on The Build:

  • GodHand Ultimate Nipper 5.0
  • Bauer 800 Grit Sandpaper
  • Bauer 1200 Grit Sandpaper
  • Exacto Knife
  • Tweezers
  • Polishing Cloth

Thoughts:

I built this kit shortly after building the Schwarzette model kit. It was fairly simple, which allowed be to complete it within three days. The only issue I had with the kit was the blue stickers on the chest were difficult for me to put on and come off very easily.
