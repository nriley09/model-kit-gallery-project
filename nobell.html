HGFC Gundam Nobell Berserker

Tools I Used on The Build:

  • GodHand Ultimate Nipper 5.0
  • Bauer 800 Grit Sandpaper
  • Bauer 1200 Grit Sandpaper
  • Exacto Knife
  • Tweezers
  • Polishing Cloth

Thoughts:

I enjoyed building this kit! It was smaller than the other high grade kits I have built so far which gave me quite a challenge. I had to adapt to the size difference and became a better builder afterwards. The Nobell Berserker also came with a model base, which is something the previous kits I built lacked.
