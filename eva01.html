RG Evangelion Unit-01

Anime: Neon Genesis Evangelion

Tools I Used on The Build:

  • GodHand Ultimate Nipper 5.0
  • Bauer 800 Grit Sandpaper
  • Bauer 1200 Grit Sandpaper
  • Exacto Knife
  • Tweezers
  • Polishing Cloth

Thoughts:

This is the second Real Grade model kit I built! The building process was very similar to a Gundam; however, it did not have a waist part that needed to be built. It ended up being a fun build that did not have any problems I ran into!

Completed Parts:

  • Head Piece
  • Torso
  • Left Arm
  • Right Arm
  • Waist
  • Left Leg
  • Right Leg
  • Sword
  • Shield
