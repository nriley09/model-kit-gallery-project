HG Gundam Vidar

Tools I Used on The Build:

  • Generic Nipper off of Amazon
  • 600 Grit Sanding Stick
  • Tweezers
  • Polishing Cloth

Thoughts:

I was having trouble sleeping at when I built this model kit, which resulted in me making several mistakes throughout the building process of this kit. The biggest issue I have with this kit was the back pieces are very lose and the torso leaning due to old age. Although there are a couple improvement that could be made to my personal kit, I liked how the kit turned out.
