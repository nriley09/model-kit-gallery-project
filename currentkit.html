Thoughts:

This is the first Master Grade Gundam I have decided to build! The build for the Blaze Zaku Phantom has been surprisingly easy so far, and not very complex. The Zaku line of mechs from the Gundam series is my all-time favorite, so I decided it would be appropriate to build one as my first Master Grade model kit. I will update this section as I progress through the build!
