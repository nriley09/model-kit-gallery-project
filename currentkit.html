Current Model Kit: MG Blaze Zaku Phantom (Rey Za Burrel Custom)

Purchase Information:

Purchase Location

I purchased this kit from the Premium Bandai website.

Cost

The kit cost me $77.00 and I was able to get free shipping on the order because they had a deal for free shipping on orders more than $50

Shipping

I purchased the kit on August 23, 2025 and it arrived at my house two weeks later

How long did it take me to build?

It took me around 6 months to build because I was incredibly busy between work and school; however, I spent around 30 hours altogether building it

Tools I am Using on The Build:

  • GodHand Ultimate Nipper 5.0
  • Bauer 800 Grit Sandpaper
  • Bauer 1200 Grit Sandpaper
  • Exacto Knife
  • Tweezers
  • Polishing Cloth
  • Pink Paint
  • Paint Brush

Thoughts:

This is the first Master Grade Gundam I have decided to build! The build for the Blaze Zaku Phantom has been surprisingly easy so far, and not very complex. The Zaku line of mechs from the Gundam series is my all-time favorite, so I decided it would be appropriate to build one as my first Master Grade model kit. It turned out to be a very fun build! I only had issues with the shoulder pieces as they would become loose very easily if i rotated the shields to far. The kit has great articulation, but struggles with limited posability because of the bulkiness of the figure.
